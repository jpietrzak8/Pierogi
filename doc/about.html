Pierogi

A Universal Infrared Remote Control

Version 1.1.38 - For a Few Pierogies More

A tasty little app for controlling all your infrared devices. For more information, please visit the Pierogi website.

Pierogi is a free, open-source program. No fee, no ads, you may use it as you like. However, if you would like to contribute to the future of this app, I have set up a donations site. All funds received will be put towards the purchase of additional hardware for development work. Thank you!
