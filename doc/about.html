Pierogi

A Universal Infrared Remote Control

Version 1.1.30 - For a Few Pierogies More

A tasty little app for controlling all your infrared devices. For more information, please visit the Pierogi website.

Pierogi is a free and open-source program! Use it however you like. But if you wish to send a few appreciative dollars my way, I do accept donations. All funds received will be put towards the purchase of another N900 for development work. Thank you!
